Disney’s Live-Action Tangled Adds ‘Big Nose’ to Growing Cast
By Borys Kit | February 19, 2026 11:02 AM PST

EXCLUSIVE: Disney’s kingdom of Corona is getting a little rowdier.

Dylan Miller has joined the cast of the studio’s live-action Tangled, taking on the role of Big Nose, one of the intimidating yet unexpectedly soft-hearted ruffians who populate the Snuggly Duckling tavern.

The project already stars Teagan Croft as Rapunzel and Milo Manheim as charming outlaw Flynn Rider. The story centers on the sheltered princess with magical hair who escapes her tower with a wanted thief and embarks on an adventure that leads to the truth about her identity — and the floating lanterns she’s long been drawn to.

Sources say the remake significantly expands the pub thugs beyond their musical introduction, weaving them throughout the journey as recurring allies. Big Nose in particular becomes a reluctant protector for Flynn as royal guards close in.

The live-action version leans into a warmer ensemble dynamic, giving the Snuggly Duckling crew emotional stakes in Rapunzel’s story while keeping the humor intact.

Michael Gracey directs from a script by Jennifer Kaytin Robinson, with Kristin Burr producing. The film remains a musical and will feature new arrangements of Alan Menken’s songs alongside additional material written for the adaptation.

Production is expected to begin later this year in the U.K.
